TV Shows


Gin no Saji (Silver Spoon)




Silver spoon is a slice of life anime that focuses on the life of a student who lives in the city but went to an agricultural school in a province. This show is relaxing to watch, and explore the agricultural world through anime. It has comedy in it and which makes the show more entertaining for me. There are no beautiful fight scenes, overpowered main character, and superpowers. The show is just like about living, studying, making friends, and face different kinds of challenges. Some of the challenge are kind of relatable which makes it more interesting to watch. It is a show that is very down to Earth.

Anime Studio: A-1 Pictures
Author: Hiromu Arakawa




Barakamon



Barakamon is a slice of life anime that focuses of the main character, whose profession is a calligrapher, that moved to a peaceful island compared to Tokyo after causing some problems. What I like with this show is that watchers can see how the main character adapt and became more open to the people that he did not like at first. Also, the children in the show acts like a children which is entertaining to watch. Barakamon shows that oneself should be creative or do things outside of the box. There are time when the contents of the book will not help. One more thing that the show showed is that there are people who will help you in times of need.

Anime Studio: Kinema Citrus
Author: Yoshino, Satsuki


Movies


Josee, the Tiger and The Fish




Josee, the Tiger and the Fish is an animated movie about the love story of two characters. The show shows how love can enter one's life. Unexpected events sometimes leads to happiness. Sometimes to become happy, one needs to face his/her fear. To avoid having limited options, one must go out, and see the world, leaving the comfort zone that you stayed in a very long time. A single person can change one's life into a positive one or negative one. There are times where decisions must be made, and we should not regret choosing what we will choose.

Anime Studio: Bones
Author: Seiko Tanabe





Osomatsu-san The Movie




The movie shows the past of the sextuplets from the Osomatsu-san series. This movie may not be impactful for those who do not watched the series. The movie revealed some of the past events of the sextuplets, and a new character who likes the sextuplets. This movie tells how we should not let something be, and regret it in the future. Not only that, it also tell how important to reconcile with each other because there are other people that are affected because of the fight. People should create happy memories while they can still do it.
